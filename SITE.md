# SITE.md - Aethelon Furniture Store

## 1. Vision

**Aethelon** is an award-winning premium furniture e-commerce experience featuring:
- Scroll-driven particle animations that morph into furniture shapes
- 3D product previews with real-time material customization
- AI-powered room visualization (try furniture in your space)
- 20+ product category pages for deep organization
- Premium UX details: custom cursor, cinematic loading, page transitions

**Target:** Awwwards, FWA, CSS Design Awards quality
